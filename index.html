Who are we ?

Paint Your Dreams are a Kochi based painting service company with over 5 years of experience in both residential and commercial painting services. Our services are customized to your needs and executed from prep to clean-up by our professionally trained and experienced team .

How do we work ?

Matchless transparency in wall area measurements and cost of painting. Expert assistance on best wall paints, all types of wood and metal paints. Colour consultancy to help you choose colour combinations for living room, bedroom, kitchen and for every interior and exterior wall. We provide interior wall painting, exterior wall painting, texture painting, wall stencil painting, wood painting and polishing and metal painting services. Experience Paint Your Dreams seamless painting services in various locations of Kerala. Let's give your dream home the perfect return gift !

Why Do We Do It ?

The idea of Paint Your Dreams is to remove the pain from looking for local painters or services. Forget all the running around, negotiations and compromises you had to deal with old school painting contractors and local painters near me. We are removing the inefficiencies in the process of home painting process. No more is just writing on walls easy, we have made wall painting easier too. The joy of being a part of your beautiful space and the happiness of witnessing our professional painters fly for the first time, keeps us going.
